How to set this up

  1. Go to notion.so/my-integrations and create a new internal integration.
  2. Copy the integration's Internal Integration Secret and paste it below as the token.
  3. In Notion, create or pick a parent page where exported notes should land. Click Share on that page and invite your integration.
  4. Copy the page's URL — the 32-char ID at the end is your Parent Page ID.
